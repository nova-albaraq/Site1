stadium "Camp Nou"

We have made the most beautiful arena in Europe with a current seating capacity of 99354. This discovery became for us a breakthrough point for development for the next decades.

"Millau Viaduct" bridge

The Millau Viaduct is completed in 2004 (France). As of September 2022, it is the tallest bridge in the world, having a structural height of 336.4 metres (1,104 ft).

"Alqueva" Dam

The Alqueva Dam is an arch dam and the centrepiece of the Alqueva Multipurpose Project. It c is the largest dam and artificial lake (250 square kilometres (97 sq mi)) in Western Europe.
